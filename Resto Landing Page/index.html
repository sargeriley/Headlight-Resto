Frequently Asked Questions

Why do headlights get yellow, cloudy, or dim?
UV from sunlight breaks down the outer coating and plastic surface which oxidizes the light cover and turns it yellow. Tiny impacts from rocks will also slowly erode the plastic casing and cause the light to not travel in straight lines giving it a cloudy appearance.
What does the process look like?
You book an appointment with us through email, the calendar, or phone number then we’ll meet you at your house or where you want to meet. It’ll take no more than an hour to get your headlights looking brand new.
How long does the restoration take?
Most appointments take no more than one hour depending on the severity of the oxidation.
Do you come to me?
Yes! We are a mobile service. We can restore your headlights at your home or workplace anywhere in Utah County.
Will it damage my car?
Nope! The only thing I work on is the headlight covers. I also use tape to protect the paint right around the light.
How long will the clarity last?
We apply a high-grade ceramic UV sealant that typically protects the lenses for years, depending on how often the car is parked in direct sun. For reference, I did my car last September and the lights are still clear and bright.
What payments do you accept?
Cash and Venmo are both acceptable.
